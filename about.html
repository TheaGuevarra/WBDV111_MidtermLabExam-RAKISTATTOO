About Us

Rakistattoo & Body Piercing has been doing clean, quality work since 2017 — and in 2019, we found our home on the 4th floor of Ayala Malls Feliz. Whether you're coming in for a custom tattoo, a piercing, or just to vibe, we've got you. We do permanent tattoos in black-and-grey and colored styles, body piercing with full aftercare and downsizing, and henna for when you're not quite ready to commit. We cater to clients 18 and above, and we take hygiene and good work seriously — because you deserve both.
